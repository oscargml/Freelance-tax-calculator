About Freelance Tax Shield

✨ Our Mission ✨

Help freelancers, gig workers, and 1099 contractors avoid IRS penalties by making quarterly tax estimation simple, free, and accurate.

Why We Built This

In 2024, over 27 million Americans worked as freelancers. Yet most had no easy way to calculate quarterly taxes. Spreadsheets, guesswork, or expensive CPAs were the only options.

We built Freelance Tax Shield to change that — a free, browser-based calculator that gives accurate estimates in seconds. No signup. No hidden fees. Just honest numbers.
