What happens if you miss a deadline?

The IRS charges an underpayment penalty of 0.5% per month on the unpaid amount, plus interest. Use our Quarterly Tax Estimator to see if you owe.

Safe Harbor Rules (to avoid penalties)

  • Pay 90% of your current year's tax liability
  • OR pay 100% of last year's tax liability (110% if AGI over $150k)
  • If your total tax is less than $1,000, no penalty
